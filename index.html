Hello! I am Spencer West.

Freelance Web Designer & Developer based in San Diego, California.

About me

I grew up with a love for computers. My dad showed me how to use DOS and I started playing floppy disk games. I was hooked.

I graduated from Cal State University Northridge with a BA in Communication Studies. However, my passion for computers was not satisfied. I enrolled in a programming bootcamp in San Diego called Learn Academy.

I am now a full-stack web developer with the knowledge of: Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, postgresql, and more. A detailed description can be found on my LinkedIn profile.

When I am not coding I like to play Golf. I just started playing in 2016. I must say it is a frustratingly fun and humbling game. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!

Services

Design

I create low-fidelity wireframes that take UI/UX into consideration. This involves sketching, whiteboarding, and designing in the browser. I also produce high-fidelity mock ups using Photoshop.

Development

I use HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript to create customized websites. I have experience using frameworks such as Bootstrap and jQuery to keep websites mobile friendly and interactive.

WordPress

I can migrate your existing website to WordPress or create a new WordPress website. I use simplistic templates to make your ideas come to life effeciently while looking professional and modern.
